Marilyn Monroe

The Blonde Bombshell

Timeline of Marilyn Monroe's life:

  • June 1, 1926 Marilyn Monroe born Norma Jeane Mortenson in Los Angeles, California, to her mother Gladys Baker.
  • June 19, 1942 Norma Jeane marres a local 21-year-old boy, Jim Dougherty.
  • 1946 Norma Jeane goes blonde.
  • June 1946 Norma Jeane and Jim Dougherty get divorced.
  • August 25, 1946 Norma Jeane changes her name to Marilyn Monroe.
  • March 3 1950 Marx Brothers' movie "Love Happy" is released, starring Marilyn Monroe.
  • July 18, 1953 20th Century Fox releases "Gentlemen Prefer Blondes" starring Marilyn Monroe.
  • November 5, 1953 20th Century Fox releases "How to Marry a Millionaire."
  • December 1953 Hugh Hefner establishes Playboy starring Marilyn Monroe.
  • January 14, 1954 Marilyn Monroe marries Joe DiMaggio in San Francisco, California.
  • April 30, 1954 Marilyn Monroe stars in "River of No Return."
  • December 31, 1954 Marilyn Monroe Productions is formed.
  • 1955 Marilyn Monroe takes acting lessons from Actors Studios.
  • April 8, 1955 Marilyn Monroe appears on "Person to Person."
  • June 3, 1955 "The Seven Year Itch" Is Released.
  • October 31, 1955 Marilyn Monroe and Joe DiMaggio get divorced.
  • June 29, 1956 Marilyn Monroe marries Arthur Miller.
  • August 31, 1956 20th Century Fox releases "Bus Stop" also known as The Wrong Kind of Girl, starring Marilyn Monroe.
  • June 13, 1957 "The Prince and the Showgirl" is released starring Marilyn Monroe.
  • March 29, 1959 "Some Like It Hot" is released starring Marilyn Monroe.
  • March 8, 1960 Marilyn Monroe receives Golden Globe for Best Comedy Actress.
  • January 1961 Marilyn Monroe and Arthur Miller get divorced.
  • February 1, 1961 "The Misfits" is released starring Marilyn Monroe.
  • May 19, 1962 Marilyn Monroe sings "Happy Birthday, Mr. President" to John F. Kennedy.
  • August 5, 1962 Marilyn Monroe dies, due to a drug overdose.
